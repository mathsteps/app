Step by Step

MathSteps is a math calculater app. It is designed for children, students and teachers. It displays the process of calculating step by step for users, which is not only giving the calculate result, but showing how to get it.

Fraction support

MathSteps supports fraction calculate, including fraction plus, minus, multiply, division, fraction reduction and fraction simplification. It shows the process of two fractions calculate step by step for users.

2D display

As you seen from the sreenshot of MathSteps. The math displays in it is just in the way that we write math on paper. MathSteps makes the math input easyer, and the math structure between numbers showing more clearly.
